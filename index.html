VIOLA: A Framework for the Automatic Generation of Virtual Analog Audio Plug-ins based on WDFs

Audio Samples

The following audio samples are provided as supplementary material for the article VIOLA: A Framework for the Automatic Generation of Virtual Analog Audio Plug-ins based on WDFs, authored by R. Giampiccolo, S. Ravasi, and A. Bernardini, and submitted to the special issue "The Sound of Digital Audio Effects" of the Journal of the Audio Engineering Society.


The wipers of the potentiometers are set as follows:
  • Electro-Harmonix Big Muff Pi:  xG = 40% (gain),  xT = 70% (tone),  xL = 30% (level)
  • Digitech Overdrive Preamp 250:  xG = 50% (gain),  xL = 20% (level)
  • Marshall BluesBreaker:  xG = 80% (gain),  xT = 30% (tone),  xL = 70% (level)
  • MXR Distortion+:  xG = 70% (gain),  xL = 50% (level)
